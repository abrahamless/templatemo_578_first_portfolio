TAME IMPALA

FIND OUT ABOUT TAME IMPALA'S MUSIC

ABOUT

TAME IMPALA IS THE PSYCHEDELIC MUSIC PROJECT OF AUSTRALIAN MULTI-INSTRUMENTALIST KEVIN PARKER. IN THE RECORDING STUDIO, PARKER COMPOSES, RECORDS, PERFORMS AND PRODUCES ALL OF THE MUSIC ON THE PROJECT. ON TOUR, TAME IMPALA CONSISTS OF PARKER, DOMINIC SIMPER, JAY WATSON, CAM AVERY AND JULIEN BARBAG

MUSIC

DESCRIPTION

THE MUSIC OF TAME IMPALA, LED BY KEVIN PARKER, IS CHARACTERIZED BY A UNIQUE BLEND OF PSYCHEDELIA, ROCK AND ELECTRONIC POP. KNOWN FOR THEIR IMMERSIVE SOUNDSCAPES, HYPNOTIC RHYTHMS AND INTROSPECTIVE LYRICS, TAME IMPALA FUSES 60S AND 70S PSYCHEDELIC ROCK INFLUENCES WITH MODERN PRODUCTION. THEIR MUSIC INVITES YOU ON A SONIC JOURNEY THAT EXPLORES INTROSPECTION, LOVE AND THE PASSAGE OF TIME.
